SSCAlangilan
- Leading Innovation Transforming Lives Building the Nation

This organization is the highest governing student organization in BatStateU-Alangilan serving 21,000 students. These leaders thrive and aspire to serve the four(4) distint campuses of Batangas State University- Alangilan, Balayan, Lobo and Mabini Campus.

Together, we in the Red Spartan community will continue the legacy we built. Let us all strive for greatness. Leading innovations, Transforming Lives, Building the Nation.
